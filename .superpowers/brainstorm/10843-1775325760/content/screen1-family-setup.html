How it works

  • Pre-loaded with Sarah, Ruth, and Jake — demo-ready on launch
  • Each member gets a unique color (purple/blue/cyan) carried through the whole app
  • Interest tags are emoji-prefixed chips for quick scanning
  • Energy level + accessibility shown as subtle metadata below tags
  • Budget is editable with a slider — the progress bar fills as the itinerary gets built
  • Tapping a card opens an edit sheet (not shown) to modify preferences
  • "Build Our Itinerary" triggers the matching animation (Screen 2)
