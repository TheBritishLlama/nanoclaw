Key mechanics shown

  • Activity cards — time, place, cost, who it's for (avatars top-right), and personal reactions from each matched member
  • Overlap surfacing — Pike Place shows all 3 family members matched for different reasons
  • "Meanwhile" alternatives — when someone sits out, a teal/purple callout shows what they'll do instead at the same location
  • Wildcard card — dark themed, special feel. Shows the pick, the personal quote, and the satisfaction score boost
  • Satisfaction scores — persistent in the header bar, always visible
  • Day tabs — click to switch between Day 1/2/3, each with its own city
  • Budget tracker — running total in header, final total in the CTA bar
  • 2-column grid — uses widescreen space, avoids long vertical scroll on projector
