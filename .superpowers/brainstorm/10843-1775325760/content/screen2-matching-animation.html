Animation sequence (~4 seconds)

  • Phase 1 — Scatter (1s): Interest chips float out from the 3 member avatars, drifting across a dark backdrop
  • Phase 2 — Match (1.5s): Chips cluster and merge into activity cards. Member avatars attach to cards they match with
  • Phase 3 — Settle (1.5s): Cards slide into position on a 3-day timeline. Day headers fade in. Progress bar completes.
  • Dark background (#0F0B2E) makes the colored elements pop on projector
  • Subtle particle glow trails behind moving chips for visual flair
  • All CSS keyframes — no JS animation library needed
